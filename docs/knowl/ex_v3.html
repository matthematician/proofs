Worksheet Exercise 3.

Let \(V\) be a vector space, and \(v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_m \in V\) be linearly independent in \(V\text{.}\)

Prove that, for any vector \(w \not\in {\rm span}\{v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_m\}\text{,}\) the vectors

\begin{equation*} v_1+w, \; v_2+w,\; \ldots, \; v_m+w \end{equation*}

are linearly independent.
