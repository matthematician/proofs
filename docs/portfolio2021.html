Appendix A Portfolio Problems, Summer 2021

Worksheet A.1 Proofs for Module V

1.

Prove that, with the usual operations of vector addition and scalar multiplication, the set

\begin{equation*} S = \bigl\{ (x_1,x_2,x_3) \in \mathbb{R}^3 \colon x_1 -2x_2 + 3x_3 = d \bigr\} \end{equation*}

is a vector space if, and only if, \(d=0\text{.}\)

2.

Let \(V\) be a vector space, and \(S \subset V\) and \(T \subset V\) be two vector subspaces of \(V\text{.}\)

Prove that the union \(S\cup T\) is a vector subspace of \(V\) if, and only if, \(S \subset T\) or \(T \subset S\text{.}\)

3.

Let \(V\) be a vector space, and \(v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_m \in V\) be linearly independent in \(V\text{.}\)

Prove that, for any vector \(w \not\in {\rm span}\{v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_m\}\text{,}\) the vectors

\begin{equation*} v_1+w, \; v_2+w,\; \ldots, \; v_m+w \end{equation*}

are linearly independent.

4.

Let \(V\) be a vector space, and \(v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_m \in V\) be linearly independent in \(V\text{.}\)

Prove that, for any vector \(w \in V\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} {\rm dim} \; {\rm span} \{ v_1+w, \, v_2+w,\, \ldots, v_m+w\} \geq m-1. \end{equation*}
